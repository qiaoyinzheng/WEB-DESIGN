Introduction to Supply Chain Management

Supply chain management (SCM) is the process of overseeing the journey of goods and services from suppliers to customers. This involves coordinating multiple stages like sourcing, production, and distribution. A well-managed supply chain can lead to cost savings, improved product quality, and satisfied customers. SCM has seen significant transformations with technological advancements. In particular, tools powered by artificial intelligence, data analytics, and machine learning are becoming key to navigating complexities and improving efficiency. The introduction of sustainable and ethical practices has also shaped modern SCM, as companies strive to balance economic benefits with social responsibility.
