Tools And Technologies in SCM

Supply chain management (SCM) relies on a suite of specialized applications to enhance efficiency and visibility across various operations.

Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP): ERP systems integrate core business processes, providing a unified view of operations.

  • SAP SCM: Offers comprehensive supply chain functionalities, including planning, manufacturing, and logistics.
  • Oracle SCM Cloud: Provides a suite of applications to manage supply chain processes, emphasizing flexibility and scalability.
  • Microsoft Dynamics 365 Supply Chain Management: Utilizes AI and IoT to automate and optimize supply chain processes.

Warehouse Management Systems (WMS): WMS applications optimize warehouse operations, enhancing storage, retrieval, and dispatching of goods.

  • Manhattan Associates: Provides advanced warehouse management capabilities to streamline operations.
  • Blue Yonder (formerly JDA): Offers comprehensive WMS solutions focusing on inventory and labor management.

Transportation Management Systems (TMS): ERP systems integrate core business processes, providing a unified view of operations.

  • Oracle Transportation Management: Integrates planning and execution to optimize transportation networks.
  • SAP Transportation Management: Provides end-to-end visibility and control over transportation processes.

Demand Forecasting Tools: ERP systems integrate core business processes, providing a unified view of operations.

  • Offers robust statistical forecasting methods to enhance demand planning.
  • Anaplan: Provides connected planning solutions to improve forecasting accuracy.
